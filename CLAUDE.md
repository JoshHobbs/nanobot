# Nanobot

## Deployment: jelly

- **Host**: `jelly` (SSH alias, Linux x86_64)
- **Nanobot source**: `~/Code/nanobot` on jelly
- **Config file**: `~/.nanobot/config.json` on jelly
- **Run method**: `docker compose up -d` from `~/Code/nanobot`
- **Stop method**: `docker compose down` from `~/Code/nanobot`
- **Current model**: `openai/gpt-5-mini`
